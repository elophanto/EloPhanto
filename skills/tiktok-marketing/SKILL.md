## Instructions

### Trend Analysis and Strategy Development
1. Use `web_search` and `browser_navigate` to monitor trending sounds, visual effects, hashtag challenges, and viral content patterns on TikTok.
2. Research current algorithm ranking factors and optimization opportunities.
3. Analyze competitor brand content and engagement strategies using `browser_navigate`.
4. Define content pillars with 40/30/20/10 mix: educational / entertainment / inspirational / promotional.
5. Save trend research and strategy using `knowledge_write`.

### Viral Content Creation
1. **Hook in 3 Seconds**: Every video must capture attention immediately with a pattern interrupt, compelling question, or surprising visual.
2. **Trending Audio Strategy**: Select trending sounds, create original audio when appropriate, and synchronize music with visual beats.
3. **Visual Storytelling**: Use quick cuts, text overlays, visual effects, and mobile-optimized vertical format.
4. **Story Arc**: Structure videos with beginning, middle, end optimized for completion rate.
5. **Hashtag Strategy**: Mix 5-8 hashtags combining trending, niche, and branded tags.
6. All content must be mobile-first and optimized for vertical viewing.

### Algorithm Optimization
1. Maximize completion rate (full video watch percentage) -- this is the primary ranking signal.
2. Optimize engagement velocity: drive likes, comments, shares in the first hour after posting.
3. Encourage user behavior triggers: profile visits, follows, rewatches.
4. Cross-promote to other platforms to boost algorithm signals.
5. Track performance via `browser_navigate` to TikTok analytics.

### Creator Partnerships and Collaborations
1. Build influencer tier strategy:
   - Nano (1K-10K): Authentic, high-engagement niche creators
   - Micro (10K-100K): Specialized expertise with engaged audiences
   - Mid-tier (100K-1M): Broader reach with category authority
   - Macro (1M+): Mass awareness and trend-setting capability
2. Partnership models: product seeding, sponsored content, brand ambassadorships, challenge participation.
3. Track creator ROI and optimize partnership mix.
4. Use `browser_navigate` to research and connect with creators.

### UGC Campaigns and Community Building
1. Design branded hashtag challenges that encourage community participation.
2. Build brand ambassador programs with long-term exclusive partnerships.
3. Engage actively in comments, duets, and stitches to foster community.
4. Plan community management strategy with response time targets.

### TikTok Advertising
1. Optimize across ad formats: In-feed ads, Spark Ads, TopView, branded effects.
2. Design creative testing: multiple video variations per campaign.
3. Target audiences by interest, behavior, and lookalike models.
4. Track cross-platform attribution and conversion measurement.
5. Use `browser_navigate` to manage ad campaigns and review performance.

### Cross-Platform Adaptation
1. Adapt TikTok-first content for Instagram Reels and YouTube Shorts.
2. Modify aspect ratios, captions, and hashtags for each platform.
3. Coordinate posting schedules to maximize reach across platforms.
