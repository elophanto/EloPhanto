## Instructions

### Cross-Platform Strategy Development
1. Define core messaging themes that can be adapted across LinkedIn, Twitter, and other professional platforms.
2. Use `web_search` and `browser_navigate` to research competitor social strategies, industry benchmarks, and audience behavior.
3. Build a unified content calendar with platform-specific adaptations.
4. Plan content cascades: primary content on LinkedIn, adapted versions on Twitter and other platforms.
5. Design engagement loops that drive cross-platform following.
6. Save strategy using `knowledge_write`.

### LinkedIn Strategy
1. **Company Page**: Plan regular updates including employee spotlights, industry insights, product news, and company culture content.
2. **Executive Personal Branding**: Develop thought leadership content for key executives -- articles, insights, and commentary.
3. **LinkedIn Articles & Newsletters**: Create long-form content for industry authority and subscriber building.
4. **Groups & Communities**: Identify and participate in industry groups for community leadership.
5. **LinkedIn Advertising**: Plan sponsored content, InMail campaigns, and lead gen forms.
6. Use `browser_navigate` for LinkedIn engagement and analytics review.

### Twitter Strategy Integration
1. Coordinate messaging with Twitter-specific tactics (see twitter-marketing skill for detailed execution).
2. Translate LinkedIn insights into Twitter-native formats (threads, quote tweets, polls).
3. Cross-promote time-sensitive content and events across platforms.
4. Maintain consistent branded and industry hashtags across platforms.

### B2B Social Selling
1. Develop social selling playbooks for sales teams.
2. Design LinkedIn outreach sequences: connect, engage, provide value, pitch.
3. Create content that supports the sales pipeline and customer education.
4. Track social selling metrics: connection acceptance rate, response rate, pipeline contribution.

### Employee Advocacy Program
1. Design ambassador programs that empower employees to share brand content.
2. Create shareable content packages with suggested copy for different platforms.
3. Set participation targets (30%+ of employees) and track engagement.
4. Provide training on personal branding and professional social media use.

### Thought Leadership Development
1. Build CEO/founder authority through consistent publishing across platforms.
2. Create timely industry commentary on trends and news.
3. Leverage social presence for speaking opportunities (conferences, podcasts).
4. Document achievements for industry recognition and award nominations.

### Campaign Management
1. Set clear objectives aligned with business outcomes per platform.
2. Define audience segmentation with platform-specific targeting.
3. Develop platform-adapted creative assets and messaging.
4. Coordinate publishing schedules across all channels.
5. Optimize ad spend allocation per platform based on performance.

### Performance Analysis
1. Use `browser_navigate` to review native analytics for each platform.
2. Build unified reporting on reach, engagement, and conversions across platforms.
3. Run A/B tests on content format, timing, and messaging.
4. Conduct competitive benchmarking on share of voice and performance.
5. Save reports using `knowledge_write`.

### Tools Reference
- `web_search` for competitor research, industry benchmarks, trending topics
- `browser_navigate`, `browser_click`, `browser_type_text` for platform engagement and analytics
- `knowledge_write` for persisting strategies, campaign plans, and performance data
- `knowledge_search` for retrieving brand guidelines and previous campaign results
- `email_send` for employee advocacy content distribution
