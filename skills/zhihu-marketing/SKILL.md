## Instructions

### Topic and Expertise Positioning
1. Identify 3-5 core topics where the brand has genuine, defensible expertise.
2. Use `web_search` and `browser_navigate` to research existing expert answers, question trends, and audience expectations on Zhihu.
3. Define a unique angle, perspective, or value-add versus existing experts.
4. Analyze competitor authority positions and identify differentiation gaps.
5. Save topic strategy using `knowledge_write`.

### Question Identification and Answer Strategy
1. Use `browser_navigate` to identify high-value questions through search, trending topics, and follower activity.
2. Evaluate questions using impact criteria: alignment with business goals (lead generation, authority, engagement).
3. Develop answer templates for comprehensive, persuasive responses.
4. Design subtle, valuable CTAs that drive website visits or lead capture -- never hard sell.
5. Only answer questions where you have genuine expertise (credibility is everything on Zhihu).

### High-Impact Content Creation
1. Write comprehensive answers (minimum 300 words) with data, research, examples, and case studies.
2. Include relevant images, screenshots, tables, and infographics for clarity and visual appeal.
3. Optimize for internal search: strategic keyword placement, heading structure, bold text for readability.
4. Include credibility signals: credentials, experience, case studies, data sources.
5. Design answers that prompt discussion and follow-up questions.
6. Maintain professional, authoritative tone while being accessible and educational.
7. Never use aggressive sales language; let expertise and value speak for itself.
8. Use `browser_navigate`, `browser_click`, `browser_type_text` for Zhihu interaction.

### Column Development and Authority Building
1. Define a unique column topic that builds ongoing thought leadership.
2. Plan a 6-month rolling content calendar with themes and publishing schedule.
3. Publish regularly (1-2 per week) to maintain subscriber engagement.
4. Engage column subscribers through comments and follow-up discussions.
5. Target 500-2,000 new column subscribers per month.

### Relationship Building and Amplification
1. Build connections with other Zhihu experts and opinion leaders.
2. Seek collaboration opportunities: co-answer questions, cross-promote content, guest columns.
3. Leverage Zhihu Live for deeper engagement with the most interested followers.
4. Compile best answers into published "Books" for additional authority signals.
5. Participate actively in discussions and build community presence.

### Performance Analysis and Optimization
1. Conduct monthly performance review via `browser_navigate`: upvote trends, visibility, engagement patterns.
2. Identify which topics and questions drive the best business results.
3. Analyze top-performing answers and replicate success patterns.
4. Track lead quality: which content sources generate qualified leads and business impact.
5. Adjust focus topics, column content, and engagement strategies based on data.
6. Save performance data using `knowledge_write`.

### Tools Reference
- `web_search` for topic research, competitor analysis, trending question discovery
- `browser_navigate`, `browser_click`, `browser_type_text` for Zhihu interaction and analytics
- `knowledge_write` for persisting topic strategies, answer logs, and performance data
- `knowledge_search` for retrieving previous answers, strategies, and brand positioning
