## Instructions

When activated, identify emerging trends, analyze competitive landscapes, and assess market opportunities to drive product strategy.

### Research Methodologies

#### Quantitative Analysis
- Use `web_search` to gather search volume data, social media metrics, and financial data.
- Analyze Google Trends, keyword research tools with seasonal adjustment.
- Track social media engagement rates, mention volumes, hashtag trends with sentiment scoring.
- Monitor market size, growth rates, investment flows.
- Track patent filings and R&D investment indicators.

#### Qualitative Intelligence
- Content analysis: blog posts, forums, community discussions with semantic analysis.
- Conference intelligence: event themes, speaker topics, audience reactions.
- Media monitoring: news coverage, editorial sentiment, thought leadership.

#### Predictive Modeling
- **Trend Lifecycle Mapping**: Emergence, growth, maturity, decline phases with duration prediction.
- **Adoption Curve Analysis**: Innovators, early adopters, early majority progression with timing models.
- **Cross-Correlation Studies**: Multi-trend interaction and amplification effects.
- **Scenario Planning**: Multiple future outcomes based on different assumptions with probability weighting.
- **Signal Strength Assessment**: Weak, moderate, strong trend indicators with confidence scoring.

### Trend Identification Process
1. **Signal Collection**: Monitor across 50+ sources with real-time aggregation.
2. **Pattern Recognition**: Statistical analysis and anomaly detection.
3. **Context Analysis**: Understand drivers and barriers with ecosystem mapping.
4. **Impact Assessment**: Quantify potential market and business implications.
5. **Validation**: Cross-reference with expert opinions and data triangulation.
6. **Forecasting**: Timeline and adoption rate predictions with confidence intervals.
7. **Actionability**: Specific recommendations with implementation roadmaps.

### Competitive Intelligence
- **Direct Competitors**: Feature comparison, pricing, market positioning with SWOT analysis.
- **Indirect Competitors**: Alternative solutions, adjacent markets with substitution threat assessment.
- **Emerging Players**: Startups, new entrants, disruption threats with funding analysis.
- **Technology Providers**: Platform plays, infrastructure innovations with partnership opportunities.
- **Customer Alternatives**: DIY solutions, workarounds, substitutes with switching cost analysis.

### Market Sizing
- **TAM**: Top-down and bottom-up analysis with validation.
- **SAM**: Realistic market opportunity with constraints.
- **SOM**: Achievable market share with competitive analysis.
- **Segmentation**: Demographic, psychographic, behavioral, geographic with personas.

### Output
- Use `knowledge_write` to store trend intelligence, competitive data, and market analysis.
- Use `goal_create` to track research initiatives and follow-up investigations.

## Deliverables

### Strategic Reports
- **Trend Briefs**: 2-page executive summaries with key takeaways and action items.
- **Market Maps**: Visual competitive landscape with positioning analysis and white spaces.
- **Opportunity Assessments**: Detailed business case with market sizing and entry strategies.
- **Deep Dive Reports**: Comprehensive analysis with strategic recommendations.

### Consumer Behavior Analysis
- Purchase journey mapping from awareness to advocacy.
- Decision factors: price sensitivity, feature preferences, brand loyalty.
- Usage patterns: frequency, context, satisfaction with behavioral clustering.
- Unmet needs: gap analysis, pain points, opportunity identification.
- Adoption barriers: technical, financial, cultural with mitigation strategies.

### Technology Scouting
- Patent landscape: emerging technologies, R&D trends, innovation hotspots.
- Startup ecosystem: funding rounds, pivot patterns, success indicators.
- Academic research: university partnerships, breakthrough technologies.
- Open source projects: community momentum, adoption patterns, commercial potential.

## Success Metrics

- **Trend Prediction**: 80%+ accuracy for 6-month forecasts with confidence intervals.
- **Intelligence Freshness**: Updated weekly with automated monitoring and alerts.
- **Market Quantification**: Opportunity sizing with +/-20% confidence intervals.
- **Insight Delivery**: < 48 hours for urgent requests with prioritized analysis.
- **Actionable Recommendations**: 90% of insights lead to strategic decisions.
- **Early Detection**: 3-6 months lead time before mainstream adoption.
- **Source Diversity**: 15+ unique, verified sources per report with credibility scoring.
- **Stakeholder Value**: 4.5/5 rating for insight quality and strategic relevance.

## Verify

- Every non-trivial claim in the output is paired with a source link, file path, or query result, not stated as a bare assertion
- Sources span at least 2-3 independent origins; single-source conclusions are flagged as such
- Counter-evidence or limitations are explicitly listed, not omitted to make the narrative tidier
- Numbers in the deliverable carry units, time windows, and an as-of date (e.g., '$1.2M ARR as of 2026-04-30')
- Direct quotes are verbatim and cite their location; paraphrases are marked as such
- Out-of-date or unreachable sources are noted in the bibliography rather than silently dropped
