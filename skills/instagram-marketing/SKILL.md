## Instructions

### Visual Brand Development
1. Use `browser_navigate` to analyze competitor Instagram accounts, noting aesthetic choices, content mix, and engagement patterns.
2. Develop a brand aesthetic guide: color palette, typography, photography style, graphic elements.
3. Plan grid layout for cohesive 9-post preview optimization.
4. Create templates for story highlights, post layouts, and graphic elements.
5. Save brand aesthetic guide using `knowledge_write`.

### Multi-Format Content Strategy
1. Follow the 1/3 rule: Brand content, Educational content, Community content.
2. **Feed Posts**: Plan single images, carousels, and video content with optimized captions and hashtags.
3. **Stories**: Design behind-the-scenes content, interactive elements (polls, questions, quizzes), and shopping integration.
4. **Reels**: Create content using trending audio, educational formats, and entertainment that balances brand authenticity.
5. **IGTV**: Plan long-form content strategy with cross-promotion from feed and stories.
6. Use `web_search` to identify trending audio, hashtags, and content formats.

### Hashtag Strategy
1. Research hashtags using `web_search` and `browser_navigate` to analyze competitor usage and trending tags.
2. Build a mix of popular (500K-5M posts), niche (10K-500K posts), and branded hashtags.
3. Create hashtag sets for different content types and rotate to avoid algorithm penalties.
4. Track hashtag performance and adjust strategy monthly.

### Instagram Shopping Integration
1. Optimize product catalog with multiple angles, lifestyle shots, and detail views.
2. Implement strategic shopping tag placement in posts and stories.
3. Design cross-selling integration with related product recommendations.
4. Track shopping conversion rates via `browser_navigate` to Instagram Insights.

### Community Building and Engagement
1. Implement 2-hour response time target for comments and DMs.
2. Design UGC campaigns with branded hashtag challenges and customer spotlight programs.
3. Plan live sessions for Q&A, product launches, and behind-the-scenes content.
4. Build micro-influencer and brand ambassador partnerships.
5. Use `browser_navigate` for active engagement on the platform.

### Algorithm Optimization
1. Maximize first-hour engagement (Golden Hour Strategy) through timing and engagement prompts.
2. Cross-promote: use Stories to drive feed post engagement, create IGTV trailers.
3. Optimize for relationship, interest, timeliness, and usage algorithm factors.
4. Track performance via `browser_navigate` to Instagram analytics.
5. Save performance reports using `knowledge_write`.

### Tools Reference
- `web_search` for trend research, hashtag analysis, competitor discovery
- `browser_navigate`, `browser_click`, `browser_type_text` for platform interaction and analytics
- `knowledge_write` for persisting aesthetic guides, content calendars, and performance data
- `knowledge_search` for retrieving brand guidelines and previous campaign data
